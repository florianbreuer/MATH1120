Let \(A\) and \(B\) be appropriately sized matrices. Then
  1. \(\displaystyle (A^T)^T = A\)
  2. \(\displaystyle (A+B)^T = A^T + B^T \)
  3. \(\displaystyle (kA)^T = k(A^T) \)
  4. \(\displaystyle (AB)^T = B^TA^T \)
  5. \((A^r)^T = (A^T)^r \) where \(r \in \mathbb{N}\text{.}\)
