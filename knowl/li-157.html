Item b.
The time when the amount of isotope has decayed to \(50\) milligrams is the value of \(t\) that satifies
\begin{equation*} 50 = 100 e^{-kt}\text{,} \end{equation*}
which is
\begin{equation*} t = \dfrac{\ln(0.5)}{-k} = \dfrac{\ln(2)}{k} \approx 24.5 \, \text{ days}\text{.} \end{equation*}
