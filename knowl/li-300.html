Item 2.
For the \(n\times n\) matrix \(A=\begin{pmatrix} a_{ij} \end{pmatrix}\) we define the minor, \(M_{ij} \) as the determinant of the \((n-1)\times (n-1) \) matrix obtained by deleting the \(i\)th row and the \(j\)th column of \(A \text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \det(A)=\sum_{j=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+j}a_{ij}M_{ij}\qquad \text{for any}\qquad i=1,2,3,\ldots,n \end{equation*}
or
\begin{equation*} \det(A)=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+j}a_{ij}M_{ij}\qquad \text{for any}\qquad j=1,2,3, \ldots, n. \end{equation*}
