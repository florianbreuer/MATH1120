Algorithm 11.7. Solving a First Order Linear DE.

To solve the first order linear DE
\begin{equation*} y' + P(x) y = Q(x) \end{equation*}
  1. Calculate the integrating factor
    \begin{equation*} I(x) = e^{\int P(x) dx} \end{equation*}
  2. Multiply both sides of the DE by the integrating factor \(I(x)\)
  3. Solve the resulting exact DE \(\dfrac{d}{dx} \left(I(x) y \right) = I(x) Q(x)\)
