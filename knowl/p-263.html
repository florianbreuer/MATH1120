The contours (i.e. level curves) of a function satisfy the equation \(z=k\text{.}\) So for this function the level curves are:
\begin{equation*} x^2+y^2=k \end{equation*}
that is, circles centred on the origin and whose radius is \(\sqrt{k}\text{.}\) Notice that there are no level curves when \(k\lt 0\text{.}\) This tells us that the surface does not go below the \(xy\)-plane.
